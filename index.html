OUR FACILITIES

Bed & Breakfast

Rated as the highest in the large local area in North Tyne Valley, we are proud to announce with our customer satisfaction rate the ultimate Bed & breakfast where you can enjoy time yourself or with your loved ones. If you are looking for a romantic gateaway in the countryside, look no furthur as we also provides equipments to activities around here such as hiking, mountain climbing. Our breakfast menu includes continental breakfast with condiments from all around the world.

General Store

Our General Store here will provide you with all the basic supplies you will find in most supermarkets whereas only difference being is we support our local economy and moreover our farmers hence investing into the town more. We open same as any of our other services and most of the food is organic and freshly grown.

Craft Shop

Chollerton Tearooms is much more than just a tea room. One of the many services we offer which you may struggle to find elsewhere in the local area is a craft shop. This is a very welcome addition to our services and was added after great demand from the local community. If you are feeling artsy, you will find a large range of supplies at Chollerton Tearoom's craft shop.
